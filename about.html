About Me

Who I Am

Hi! I’m Romeo Garcia Jr., a Mexican-American engineer who’s passionate about robotics, automation, and building innovative solutions. Right now, I’m finishing up my master’s in Mechanical Engineering, focusing on mechatronics and automatic controls. Before this, I earned my BS in Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in product realization.

Along the way, I’ve had the chance to work on some amazing projects, like spending two summers at a startup figuring out how to respond to aerodynamic changes in real time for semi-trucks. Some of my interests include sustainable mobility, green technologies, and coming up with ideas that help shape a better, more sustainable future.

I love tackling tough problems, working with others on creative projects, and bringing ideas to life. Whether it’s talking about sustainable transportation, mobility, or the latest green tech ideas, I’m always up for a good conversation. Let’s connect!

What I Do

I specialize in designing and building autonomous systems, combining advanced robotics, machine learning, and embedded systems. My focus is on solving real-world problems through innovative solutions, from creating smart robotic arms to programming complex control systems.

What I Aspire to Do

I aim to revolutionize industries by leveraging cutting-edge robotics and AI technologies. Whether it's creating sustainable solutions, developing autonomous systems, or innovating in the field of automation, my ultimate goal is to make a meaningful impact on the world.
